ABOUT ME

...

Hi! My name is Christopher Stewart. I am a multi-skilled individual based in the UK. My passions are for art, programming, software design, content creation and video-games.

My skills include but aren't limited to:
- Python and Java development
- Web design and development
- Video-game design and development
- UI design
- Pixel art and character design
- Video editing


To contact me for any reason, please email me at
coloursplashbusiness@gmail.com

ACCOMPLISHMENTS

...

I have previously created, developed and hosted a Minecraft mini-game server called "The Beginning" on my own along with a website, online store and forums. Gamemodes included multiple duels modes, kit PvP and skywars, all done with a custom-coded plugin. Unfortunately, the server shut down in July 2019 after a year.

Currently working as a moderator on the Pyrelic Network, I have over 5 years of experience organising, communicating with and developing for online communities.

As a content creator, I am also running a Discord community, Twitch and YouTube channel. Links to these are in the socials section below.

Mei, a Discord bot, is the largest project I have worked on to date.
